## Governance

This project is maintained by the Premix ORM maintainers.

Decision making
- Changes are proposed via issues or pull requests.
- Decisions are made by consensus among maintainers.
- In case of disagreement, maintainers may vote; majority wins.

Maintenance
- Maintainers may add or remove maintainers as needed.
- Inactive maintainers may be moved to emeritus status.

Releases
- Releases are tracked in `CHANGELOG.md`.
- Security fixes follow `SECURITY.md`.
